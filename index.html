📍 Prepositions of Place

Joy predloglari — Handout / Tarqatma

1. What are Prepositions of Place?

Words we use to show the position or location of something.

Gapda joy yoki makonni ko'rsatish uchun ishlatiladigan so'zlar.

2. Key Prepositions | Asosiy predloglar

Preposition Meaning When to use / Qachon ishlatiladi
in ichida inside a closed space / yopiq makon ichida
on ustida, sirtida on a surface / sirtida
under ostida directly below / to'g'ridan to'g'ri ostida
next to yonida beside / biror narsaning yonida
between orasida in the middle of two things / ikkita narsaning orasida
behind orqasida at the back of something / orqasida
in front of oldida at the front of something / oldida
above balandroq higher, not touching / balandroq, lekin tegmaydi
over ustida (harakat) above with movement/covering / ustida, ko'pincha harakat bor
below pastda lower than something / pastda

3. Important Differences | Muhim farqlar

ABOVE vs OVER

Above: higher, but NOT touching
balandroq, lekin tegmaydi

Over: above, often with movement or covering
ustida, ko'pincha harakat bilan

📌 The clock is above the door.

📌 The plane flies over the city.

BELOW vs UNDER

Below: in a lower position
pastda

Under: directly below, covered by something
to'g'ridan to'g'ri ostida

📌 The valley is below the mountain.

📌 The cat is under the table.

4. Where in the sentence? | Gapda qayerda keladi?

Prepositions of place usually come at the beginning or end of a sentence. They specify the location and can completely change the meaning of the sentence.

Odatiy joy gap boshida yoki oxirida. Joy yoki makonni aniqlaydi, gap ma'nosini o'zgartiradi.
